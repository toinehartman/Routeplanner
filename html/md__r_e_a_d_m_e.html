Routeplanner

This is a routeplanner, whick gives the shortest route between two points on an 5 by 5 grid.

Synopsis

This routeplanner find a route between a starting checkpoint on the edge of a 5 by 5 grid and one or more endpoints. It can go by the checkpoints in a given order or via the shortest route that visits them all.

Code Example

``` ➜ ./bin/routeplanner.out 1 5 

(0, 4)   9:(1, 4)   8:(2, 4)   7:(3, 4)     (4, 4)

10:(0, 3) (1, 3) (2, 3) (3, 3) 6:(4, 3) 11:(0, 2) (1, 2) (2, 2) (3, 2) 5:(4, 2) 12:(0, 1) (1, 1) (2, 1) (3, 1) 4:(4, 1) (0, 0) 1:(1, 0) 2:(2, 0) 3:(3, 0) (4, 0)

Start 1: (1, 0) End 5: (4, 2)

(1, 0) ▷ (2, 0) ▷ (3, 0) ▷ (4, 0) ▷ (4, 1) ▷ (4, 2) ```

Motivation

This program wa written to guide an autonomous robot, as part of EPO2, a project of Electrical Engineering at the University of Technology Delft.

Installation

``` cd <download_folder>/Routeplanner\ Toine/ make `` And then./bin/routeplanner.out 1 5`, this will give the shortest route from checkpoint 1 to checkpoint 5.
